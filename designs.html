Designs
BedroomThis bedroom design aims to create a peaceful retreat from the outside world. The use of satin fabrics, natural wood parquet, and soft lighting help create a calming atmosphere. Neutral tones are accented with luxurious textures, providing both comfort and sophistication. Every detail is designed to promote rest and rejuvenation.
BathroomThis bathroom design combines modern materials like marble, ceramics, and plywood to create a timeless, elegant space. Strategic lighting and a minimalist approach enhance both the functionality and serenity of the room, offering a luxurious and relaxing experience.
LivingroomThe design of this living room emphasizes a balance of modern minimalism and warmth. With a focus on natural materials like walnut wood and kenaf linen, the space exudes tranquility and elegance. The use of soft lighting helps accentuate the clean lines and warm tones, creating an inviting atmosphere.
KitchenThis modern kitchen focuses on the beauty of simplicity. Neutral colors combined with sleek finishes and functional design elements create a welcoming yet practical space. The clever use of integrated storage maximizes space without sacrificing aesthetic appeal.
CafeInspired by principles of simplicity and order, the material palette emphasizes natural textures and unpretentious forms. Wooden flooring and seating, paired with soft neutrals and a gentle lighting scheme, establish a minimal environment rich in detail. The core idea is to create stillness within flow — each table feels private yet part of a unified whole. Platforms and vertical elements are designed to evoke a tactile, immersive spatial experience that goes beyond the visual.
MediaroomThe design of this media room focuses on creating an immersive and comfortable environment. Soft carpet flooring and leather wall treatments add texture, while the use of muted colors enhances the cozy, relaxed atmosphere. Strategic lighting placements ensure a cinema-like experience, with every corner carefully designed for optimal sound and visual engagement.
LandscapeThese villas offer a harmonious blend of architecture and nature, where living is not just about shelter, but about experience. The design prioritizes natural light and greenery, creating homes that breathe with their environment. Wide openings, lush courtyards, and strategic skylights allow sunlight to become a daily presence — soft in the morning, golden by dusk. The integration of landscape and architecture encourages a fluid relationship between inside and out, inviting residents to live in sync with nature.
